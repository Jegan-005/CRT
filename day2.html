Angelas's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 21/4 teaspoon yeast
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 11/2 tablespoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup butter

Instructions

  1. mix the milk
  2. melt the butter
  3. add flour in that
  4. transfer the dough
  5. knead the dough for 10mins
